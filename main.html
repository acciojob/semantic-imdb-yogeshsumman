Summary

*The Shawshank Redemption* is a 1994 drama film based on Stephen King's novella. It follows Andy Dufresne, a banker wrongfully convicted of murdering his wife and her lover, who is sentenced to life in Shawshank State Penitentiary. Over the years, Andy forms a friendship with fellow inmate Red, navigates the harsh realities of prison life, and secretly works on a plan that leads to an unexpected and inspiring conclusion.

Actors

The film stars Tim Robbins as Andy Dufresne, a quiet and intelligent man who finds hope in the darkest of places. Morgan Freeman plays Ellis "Red" Redding, a seasoned inmate and the film's narrator, whose friendship with Andy becomes the heart of the story. Other notable actors include Bob Gunton as the corrupt Warden Norton and William Sadler as Heywood.
